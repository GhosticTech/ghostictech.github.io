About us

We are a team of developers dedicated to creating open source projects that anyone can freely look at, modify, or collaborate on. Additionally, we develop mini-games on our public Minecraft server at minehut.ghostic.tech, featuring custom game modes designed for 1.8.9 PvP, such as FlatBridge.

If you're interested in applying for a Plugin Developer position, you can reach out to us at support@ghostic.tech. As a non-profit organization, we do not expect donations or provide monetary compensation freely.

Join us in the code world and explore the possibilities!

What Sets Us Apart

Discover the Ghostic community, a gathering of passionate developers dedicated to fostering creativity and collaboration. While we may not offer traditional services, we provide a unique space for enthusiasts to connect, share ideas, and explore the world of coding.

Dive into our public Minecraft server at minehut.ghostic.tech, featuring custom game modes like FlatBridge, designed for 1.8.9 PvP enthusiasts. Join us for an engaging and enjoyable gaming experience!
